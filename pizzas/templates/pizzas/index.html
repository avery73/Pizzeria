About Us
Johnny Brusco's was founded in 2004 by Jennifer and Robert Brusco. Robert's dad, Johnny, had a love for pizzas and always wanted to open up his own shop. Robert and Jennifer decided to make Johnny's vision come to life and open up their own store. Since opening, Johnny Brusco's has been a roaring success and has been serving the state's best pizza ever since.
